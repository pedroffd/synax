The Sovereign Protocol

00

Discover

Explore all available commands, settings, and skills. Synax is designed for transparency and developer discovery.

npx synax-cli@latest --help
01

Initialize

Establish the bridge. Synax bootstraps your context files (`AGENTS.md`, `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md`) and detects your stack.

npx synax-cli@latest init
02

Augment

Choose specialized expertise. Inject personas for architecture, security, or design directly into your agent's knowledge base.

npx synax-cli@latest add
03

Bridge

Consolidate and Sync. Map all skills and rules to your target environment (Cursor, Claude Code) with zero friction.

npx synax-cli@latest bridge
